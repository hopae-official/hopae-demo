Gartner Security & Risk Management Summit

September 14 – 17, 2020|Americas | Virtual

Virtual
Experience

Get unparalleled, unbiased guidance, tailored to your critical priorities and challenges virtually at Gartner Security & Risk Management Summit 2020, Americas.

2020 virtual conferences

The Gartner virtual conference is designed for IT and business executives and decision makers looking to adapt and evolve their organization through disruption and uncertainty, navigate risks and prioritize investments. Gartner virtual conferences bring together forward-thinking leaders like you to explore emerging trends, accelerate your learning and guide near-term decisions.

Featuring a flexible program of live presentations, interactive clinics, roundtable discussions, leading solution providers and on-demand content, our virtual conferences provide timely access to the latest independent Gartner research and guidance as well as insights from other expert speakers. 

At a Gartner virtual conference, you will benefit from unparalleled, unbiased guidance, tailored to your critical priorities and designed to equip you to identify the optimal strategic direction, answer pivotal questions and guide decisions that need to be made in the next months to drive your organization’s future success.

Take a moment to explore the upcoming virtual Gartner Security & Risk Management Summit.

Designed to educate, inspire and engage

Live presentations

Get inspired by Gartner experts and visionary leaders while learning about the latest trends, exploring how to instantly apply these insights and gaining unique perspectives.

Interactive sessions

Engage with Gartner experts, vendors and peers in Ask the Expert, clinics and roundtables to discover answers to shared challenges.

Leading solution providers

Hear directly from leading solution providers., explore capabilities with live demos, and get unbiased Gartner insights on the vendor landscape.

On-demand content

Receive on-demand access to session content during and after the conference and explore a wide spectrum of relevant topics at your own pace.

All things virtual. All in one place.

Gartner virtual conferences are delivered within one integrated portal, allowing you to explore every aspect of the conference, plan your personal agenda, engage in the live conference and navigate sessions postconference. 

  • Explore and build your virtual conference
  • During the conference
  • After the conference

Explore and build your virtual conference

The Gartner virtual conference portal acts as your home base where you can plan your personal agenda around your requirements.

  • Plan your agenda and schedule sessions.
  • Sign up for interactive clinics and roundtables.
  • Schedule Gartner one-on-one meetings.
  • Explore a variety of special programs by role, industry and more.
  • Apply for exclusive programs with targeted content and sessions designed to facilitate interaction between Gartner experts, solution providers and peers.
  • Identify the right solutions for your needs by exploring our directory of solution providers and their product offerings.

During the conference

Use the portal to manage your conference experience in real time so you can attend scheduled sessions, interact with speakers, vendors and the universe of virtual attendees, and even change course in real time and explore new sessions.

  • Navigate the live virtual conference.
  • Engage in interactive Q&As and chats during sessions with speakers and peers.
  • Interact with Gartner experts in one-on-one meetings.
  • Participate in virtual networking sessions with other attendees.
  • Secure answers to your questions in real time or via demos by leading solution providers.

After the conference

Postconference, the portal offers access to many recorded virtual conference sessions in the on-demand library, enabling you to deep-dive on specific topics and experience the full depth of the virtual conference content when you need it.

  • Explore independent Gartner research and advise across tracks and hot topics.
  • Listen to insights and best practices from leading solution providers.
  • Hear how your peers are tackling similar priorities and challenges in case study examples.
  • Get access to presentations and links to research and resources.
  • Develop a shortlist of solution providers to help address your mission-critical priorities.
